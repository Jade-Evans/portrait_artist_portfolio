Commission Enquiries

Once you have decided on the styles, size and other attributes of your portrait, shoud you wish to continue with your purchase you can contact me via the enquiries form below. The more information you include the better, such as:

  • A copy of the order quotation above showing your options
  • A CLEAR image of the portrait N.B. To truly capture the character of the subject, 2 or 3 images is usually best. Please indicate which one will be of primary use.
  • Is the portrait a gift, for a specific occasion?
  • Are you open to the artist making suggestions should they think cropping or a certain media choice would work better?
